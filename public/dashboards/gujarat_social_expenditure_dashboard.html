Revenue expenditure denominator is total revenue expenditure (Appendix-2). For Education, Health and Social services on the Capital exp. view, "% of budget" is the item's share of total capital outlay (Appendix-4, pure asset-creating capex) rather than total capital disbursements, since disbursements also include gross discharge of internal debt, i.e. short-term borrowing rollovers that are not real spending and would otherwise swamp the ratio. Capex is total capital outlay itself; its "% of budget" is capex's share of total capital account disbursements, i.e. what fraction of the gross capital account is genuine asset creation versus debt rollover. Total govt. spending (capital view) is total capital disbursements including that debt rollover, so its "% of budget" is trivially 100% and is shown for GSDP comparison only. Own tax revenue is the state's own tax collection (Appendix-1), excluding its share of central taxes and grants; its "% of budget" denominator is total revenue receipts, i.e. the share of state income coming from its own tax effort rather than central transfers. GSDP is current-price GSDP from MoSPI Table 21. Gujarat GSDP is unavailable for 2023-24 in the source and is omitted rather than estimated.
